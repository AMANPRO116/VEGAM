ABOUT THE PROJECT
Engineering Excellence

Team Vegam is a student engineering team competing in the Formula Kart Design Challenge (FKDC). We design, fabricate, and race a kart from scratch — pushing boundaries in performance, safety, and innovation.


Our kart embodies the spirit of Vegam — a Sanskrit word meaning speed. Every component is engineered with purpose, every decision driven by data, and every lap fueled by passion.

Legacy of Excellence · 2016 – 2024

2016 – 17
BAJA SAEINDIA 2017
First time competing at BAJA SAE India Main Race. Won 2nd Prize in Cost Category — a landmark debut for Team Vegam.
🏆 2ND PRIZE · COST CATEGORY
2017 – 18
GKDC — GO KART DESIGN COMPETITION
Designed and built a Formula Go-Kart from scratch. Competed in static and dynamic events showcasing engineering depth and fabrication quality.
2018 – 19
GKDC · BUDDH INTERNATIONAL CIRCUIT
Completed all Static & Dynamic events at Buddh International Circuit, Greater Noida. Overall Rank #11 nationally out of all competing teams.
🏁 RANK #11 · NATIONALLY
2022 – 23
KART BUILD SEASON
Returned with a fully redesigned Formula Kart — new chassis, improved dynamics and performance tuning in preparation for the national circuit.
2023 – 24
FKDC SEASON 8 · KARI MOTOR SPEEDWAY
63 teams registered nationally. Only 45 qualified for the race after technical inspection. Team Vegam cleared all events and secured 8th Position nationally.
🏆 8TH POSITION · NATIONAL LEVEL
2025 – 26
SAEISS DDC — DRONE DEVELOPMENT CHALLENGE
First time competing in the SAE India Southern Section Drone Development Challenge. Team Velastra from Amrita School of Engineering, Bangalore designed and built a fixed-wing drone from scratch and secured AIR 17 in the Overall Category — a landmark milestone for the team.
