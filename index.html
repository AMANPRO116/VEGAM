ABOUT THE PROJECT
Engineering Excellence

Team Vegam is a student engineering team competing in the Formula Kart Design Challenge (FKDC). We design, fabricate, and race a kart from scratch — pushing boundaries in performance, safety, and innovation.


Our kart embodies the spirit of Vegam — a Sanskrit word meaning speed. Every component is engineered with purpose, every decision driven by data, and every lap fueled by passion.

GKDC SEASON 11 · KARI MOTOR SPEEDWAY
GKDC (Go Kart Design Challenge) is a competition initiated by ISNEE Motorsports to offer students maximum design flexibility and the freedom to express their creativity with few restrictions on overall kart design. The main objective is for students to assume they work for a firm designing, fabricating, testing and demonstrating a prototype kart for the non-professional competition market. Team Vegam participated in GKDC Season 11 conducted by ISNEE Motorsports at Kari Motor Speedway, Coimbatore.
FKDC — SEASON 8
FORMULA KART DESIGN CHALLENGE
FKDC (Formula Kart Design Challenge) is a competition initiated by FMAE (Fraternity of Mechanical and Automotive Engineers) to offer students maximum design flexibility and the freedom to express their creativity with few restrictions on overall kart design. The main objective is for students to assume they work for a firm designing, fabricating, testing and demonstrating a prototype kart for the non-professional competition market.

Team Vegam participated in FKDC Season 8 conducted by FMAE at Kari Motor Speedway, Coimbatore. The 8th FKDC is a national-level competition in which a total of 63 teams registered from all over India. Of these, only 50 teams completed fabrication and cleared in-house technical inspection, and only 45 teams cleared the technical inspection and passed all dynamic tests to qualify for the race. Team secured 8th Position.
